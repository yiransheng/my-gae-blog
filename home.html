Welcome

Yiran Sheng is a finance professional, he takes a quantitative approach to investing and specializes in trading strategy backtesting.

Yiran was born at the tail of an innocent era. He had a merry childhood in the countryside. 1990s was a decade of arcadian joy rather than an interval of mordernization - as to the rest of his nation.

His next ten years was spent on education, through which Yiran learned many interesting things. For instance, how to interpret Twin Paradox, how the security markets failed to follow a random walk, and how to be an nice humble, easy-going individual.

He writes web apps, reads fantasy literature and does graphical design and CGI in his spare time.
